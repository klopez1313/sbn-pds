MSX Infrared Minor Planet Survey

The Midcourse Space Experiment Infrared Minor Planet Survey (MIMPS) includes infrared data for 168 main-belt asteroids serendipitously observed by the Midcourse Space Experiment (MSX) in the course of its long-scan astronomy survey observations, as well as albedos and diameters derived from the data.
