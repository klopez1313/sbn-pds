OSIRIS-REx OLA Bundle

This bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Laser Altimeter (OLA). OLA is used for the topographic characterization of the surface of (101955) Bennu.

The bundle now includes all data through May 17, 2019, covering nearly through the end of Detailed Survey.

Release dates:

  • Initial release Nov. 7, 2019: Data through Feb. 17, 2019
  • Second release Dec. 12, 2019: Data through May 17, 2019

Note: This data set is certified with liens. Please review the liens list before using the data. The liens list also shows a planned timeline for resolution of the liens.
