OSIRIS-REx OCAMS Bundle

This bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Camera Suite (OCAMS). OCAMS is a suite of scientific cameras used for the characterization of the surface of (101955) Bennu.

The bundle now includes all data through Feb. 17, 2019, through Preliminary Survey. Note that OCAMS did not take data during Orbit A.

Release dates:

  • Initial release Feb. 15, 2019 with Cruise 1, EGA, and Cruise 2.
  • Second release Aug. 19, 2019 with most but not all of Approach phase.
  • Third release Nov. 4, 2019 through Preliminary Survey (no OCAMS data for Orbit A).
