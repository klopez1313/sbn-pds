OSIRIS-REx OVIRS Bundle

This bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Visible and InfraRed Spectrometer (OVIRS). OVIRS is used for the spectral characterization of the surface of (101955) Bennu.

The bundle now includes all data through Nov. 17, 2018, covering nearly through the end of Bennu Approach phase.

Release dates: Initial release Feb. 15, 2019 with Cruise 1, EGA, and Cruise 2. Second release Aug. 19, 2019 with most but not all of the Approach phase.
