Dawn at Ceres - GRaND

Raw Data (Level 1a)

The GRaND L1a are a time-ordered collection of gamma ray and neutron counting data and histograms acquired by GRaND during all phases of the Dawn mission. The dataset includes state-of-health data, such as temperature and voltage readings, needed for the analysis of the counting data. This data set covers the entire Ceres encounter through Extended Mission XM04 (Opposition orbit).

Calibrated Data (Level 1b)

The GRaND Reduced Data Records (RDR) contain a time series of calibrated spectra, counting data, and ephemeris, pointing, and geometry (EPG) data. The data set includes all L1b for the entire Ceres encounter through XMO4 (Opposition orbit).
