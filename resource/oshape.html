Small Body Optical Shape Models

This data set contains the Peter Thomas shape models for small solar system bodies, as well as image mosaics constructed from these models. Shape models for the following bodies are included: 243 Ida, 951 Gaspra, 253 Mathilde, 4 Vesta, M1 Phobos, M2 Deimos, S7 Hyperion, S10 Janus, and S11 Epimetheus.
