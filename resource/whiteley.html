Whiteley NEO Photometry

This data set includes the ECAS photometry and taxonomic types of 77 Near Earth Objects published by R.J. Whiteley in his thesis (Whiteley, 2001. A compositional and dynamical survey of the near-earth asteroids. Ph.D. thesis, University of Hawaii).
