Hayabusa Data Archive

Main target body: Asteroid (25143) Itokawa
Launch date: 9 May 2003
Earth Flyby date: 19 May 2004
Itokawa orbit operations: 12 September 2005 - 19 November 2005

Hayabusa primary instrument data:

Data type Browse/Download Description
Images Asteroid Multi-band Imaging Camera (AMICA) A 1024x1000 pixel camera with seven ECAS filters and a wide-band filter.

Near-Infrared Spectrometer (NIRS) calibrated
Nuclear X-Ray Spectrometer (XRS) Data from XRS were not archived in PDS. XRS data are available at DARTS.
Magnetometer N/A No magnetometer instrument on Hayabusa
Dust N/A No dust detector instrument on Hayabusa
Altimetry Light Detection and Ranging (LIDAR) Raw and calibrated laser altimetry data
Radio Science N/A Hayabusa Radio Science data were not released by the mission.
Ancillary Hayabusa SPICE archive Archived at NAIF
Hayabusa Groundbased calibration data Groundbased ECAS photometry of Hayabusa AMICA calibration stars
